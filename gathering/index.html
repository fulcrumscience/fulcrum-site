Gathering

AI for Science Power Users.

An intimate, dialogue-driven gathering of researchers and engineers who are actively deploying AI tools in scientific workflows. The goal: build mutual understanding, share priorities and challenges, and strengthen relationships across the ecosystem.

  • DateJune 3–4, 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM
  • LocationFulcrum Science, 450 Lexington Ave, Floor 38, New York, NY
  • AttendanceLimited to 40 participants (max 2 per organization)
Format & Discussions

Conversations, not presentations.

The format centers on small group conversations. Before the event, we'll reach out to confirmed attendees to learn which topics they're most interested in discussing.

  • Bottlenecks in getting AI tools into working research pipelines
  • Gap analysis
  • The future of agentic science education
  • Funding shortfalls for integration and infrastructure work
  • Building open benchmarks
  • Encouraging a culture of open science and shared tooling
