Fulcrum.

Leverage for Discovery

Connecting exceptional technical talent with world-leading researchers to accelerate scientific breakthroughs.

01 — Our Mission

Give me a lever long enough, and I shall move the world.

AI can compress years of scientific work into days. But most researchers don't have access to the engineering talent that makes this possible. Fulcrum places AI engineers directly with research teams.

02 — The Moment

The tools exist. The talent is scattered.

77%
AI success rate on olympiad-level problems
25%
AI success rate on PhD-level research

Source: Evaluating AI's ability to perform scientific research tasks, OpenAI, 2025

AI systems can now solve olympiad-level problems, but getting results on real research requires people who understand both the tools and the science. Most labs don't have them.
