Leverage for Discovery

We connect exceptional technical talent with world-leading researchers to accelerate scientific breakthroughs. AI is transforming what's possible. We're ensuring it transforms what matters.

01 — Our Mission

Give me a lever long enough, and I shall move the world.

AI capabilities now exist that can compress years of scientific work into days. But these tools remain disconnected from the researchers who need them most. Fulcrum Labs is the lever—connecting technical talent with scientific vision to unlock discoveries that matter.

Train

We recruit exceptional young technologists and train them in cutting-edge AI methods specifically designed for scientific applications—from automated experimentation to hypothesis generation.

Deploy

Fellows work directly with leading researchers across scientific domains, bringing AI capabilities to labs that are ready to be transformed but lack the technical expertise.

Accelerate

Each deployment is designed to achieve measurable acceleration—compressing research timelines, expanding experimental scope, and enabling discoveries that wouldn't otherwise happen.

Recent benchmarks reveal a critical asymmetry: AI systems excel at well-defined problems but struggle with open-ended research. This isn't a fundamental limitation—it's a training gap. The bridge between AI capability and scientific impact requires human translators who understand both domains.

"AI capabilities reflect who builds them. The question isn't whether AI will transform science, but who will direct that transformation—and toward what ends."
03 — The Model

AI Catalyst Fellowship

A structured program that embeds technically skilled fellows with research teams ready to leverage AI. We create force multipliers, not dependencies.

01

Selection & Training

We recruit talented technologists from diverse backgrounds and provide intensive training in AI-for-science methodologies, emphasizing practical implementation over theoretical abstraction.

02

Matched Deployment

Fellows are carefully matched with research partners based on domain alignment, project readiness, and potential for measurable impact. Each deployment has clear success metrics.

03

Cohort Learning

Regular gatherings bring fellows together with domain experts for lectures, workshops, and scenario exercises exploring the future of AI and scientific discovery.

04

Knowledge Transfer

Every engagement is designed to build lasting capability. We leave behind trained collaborators, documented methods, and research teams equipped to continue without us.
